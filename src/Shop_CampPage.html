Program Includes

6 hours on-ice training over 4 sessions

Program Description

The camp is a great way to improve skating, puck skills and battle skills before tryouts begin. Each of the four sessions will include specific instruction on skating before players are grouped into stations focusing on puck control, passing/shooting and game situations. Many team coaches use the camp as an opportunity to watch and evaluate players in a competitive environment on last time before tryouts begin. The goal is to have players leave with more skills than when they arrived and feeling confident that they're going to perform exceptionally well in their tryouts!
