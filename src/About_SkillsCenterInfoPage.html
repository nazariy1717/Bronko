Features

Keys to success

Skating is a difficult movement to duplicate off the ice. The Bronko Belt allows players to add resistance to the actual skating movements they need to perform in games and practices. In this way, players can develop muscle strenght and endurance that can be expressed in the coordinated manner necessary for powerful skating.

Detailed professional instruction
  • Attention to detail.The details make the difference between good players and elite players. Get the drills we use and the key teaching points your players need to improve technique and increase speed.
  • Correction and repetition.Players need specific, constructive feedback and then they need quality repetitions to build good habits. You can provide both using this program.
Effective drill progressions
  • A strong foundation. Most players have bad habits that can limit their development if not properly addressed. Learn to look at a player's execution like a skills coach so you can eliminate bad habits that sabotage speed and limit effectiveness.
  • Add new layers of skill. Skills Center drill progressions come with multiple options and/or variations so you can tailor the level of the drill to your players and layer new skills on top of skills already learned.
Small group training
  • Be specific. We use specific terms for all the skills and tactics used in our drills. Use the terms with your team so you can be specific about what you want your players to do and how you want them to do it.
  • Combine skills. Most skills are introduced in isolation and then new variations are layered on top. Players learn to blend skating techniques, puck control variations and tactics into intuitive, fluid movements.
