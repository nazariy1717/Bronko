Welcome to Bronko Hockey

Bronko Hockey provides exceptional, professional hockey instruction to players, coaches and teams. We provide expert training in skating, puck skills and tactics/systems. We offer a range of camps, clinics and customized training sessions with individuals, small groups and teams.

Our experienced instructors can effectively communicate, demonstrate and evaluate the skills and concepts being taught making them accessible to players and coaches at all levels. We look to build skills, knowledge and confidence in a fun and challenging environment.

We also offer our patented new training tool, the Bronko Belt RTI. It's a simple and effective way for players to develop the leg and core muscles used during skating. It's being used by a growing number of players and teams across Canada and the United States. In a few short years we've gone from introducing the Belt at our camps and clinics to having customers in 9 of 10 provinces and 23 states. To help coaches and teams maximize the benefits of training with the Belt we've begun sharing the program we've developed with interested teams and associations.
