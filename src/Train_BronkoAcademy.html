Bronko Academy Morning Program

Train with Bronko Hockey’s elite instructors at the Powerade Center in Brampton for 4 days each week during the upcoming school year! In conjunction with your school’s High Performance Program, Bronko Hockey will provide you with the best hockey instruction in the city. With Dan, Dion and Sam Del Monte your player will learn the skating techniques, puck skills and decision making needed to compete and excel at a high level.

“Thinking the game” has been a key component of the way Bronko Hockey teaches the game from the start and has been a big reason for our continuing success. The best players have high end skills and are able to make smart decisions with and without the puck all over the ice. Our goal is to help your child not only develop elite skills but the decision making and hockey sense needed to apply those skills effectively during games for maximum results.

  • 2017 - 2018 School Year
  • Powerade Center
  • Dan Del Monte 905.782.0205 admin@bronkohockey.com
COMPREHENSIVE ON AND OFF ICE PROGRAM FOR ELITE PLAYERS
  • 90 Minutes on ice each day
  • Hockey specific off ice training including Core and Balance Training, Plyometrics, Footspeed
  • Shooting station
  • Video analysis
  • Classroom teaching of team tactics and systems
  • Shuttle bus from Powerade to school is provided
HIGHLIGHTS
  • September - June / Monday - Thursday. Program runs throughout the school year
  • No Residency Rule. It does not matter where you live. You can attend the local public school from anywhere. Call for more school information.
  • Experienced Instructors. Attention to detail is key to rapid improvement.
  • Bronko Belt training included. Use Bronko Belts 1-2 times per week.
