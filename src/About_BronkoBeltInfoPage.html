About

What is the Bronko Belt?

The Bronko Belt RTI is the first and only weighted training belt specifically designed for hockey players. It is a simple and effective way for players to develop the leg and core muscles used during skating.

The Belt comfortably distributes an adjustable amount of training weight evenly around a player's center of gravity without compromising that player's ability to skate, shoot or stickhandle. Players wear the Belt over their pants during a portion of practice and are able to execute skills with full range of motion. Depending on the type of training done, players can experience improvement in a number of areas that will positively impact on-ice performance:

Single leg strength for proper knee bend

Explosive power for acceleration and agility

Core strength, dyanamic balance and stability

Anaerobic endurance

Benefits

Sport-specific training

Skating is a difficult movement to duplicate off the ice. The Bronko Belt allows players to add resistance to the actual skating movements they need to perform in games and practices. In this way, players can develop muscle strenght and endurance that can be expressed in the coordinated manner necessary for powerful skating.

Features

Built for hockey players

The patented design of the Bronko Belt RTI offers a combination of features tailored specifically to the needs of hockey players. Special consideration has been given to the high-speed, high-intensity, full-contact nature of the game and as a result the belt is able to provide hockey players with the benefits of resistance training in a sport-specific manner without compromising the player's performance, comfort or safety.

Performance

Even weight distribution around your center of gravity:

  • The belt positions the training weight around your center of gravity so your balance, range of motion and skating stride won't be negatively affected while you train. This allows you to improve skating power without compromising skating technique.
  • The belt's placement on the hips leaves your hands free while training. Players can shoot, puck handle and pass normally meaning they can wear the belt during practice including during drills that simulate game situations.
  • The curved, form-fitting weight packs of the belt hug your hips snugly on top of your pants to achieve as low a profile as possible so the belt fits inconspicuously under your jersey and doesn't get caught up on other players or the boards.
Comfort

Easy to use and fully customizable for a comfortable fit:

  • The belt can be adjusted to fit just about any player. The unique fastening system locks the weight packs in place on the waist belt and prevents them from sagging or otherwise moving while you play. The system allows you to easily adjust the positioning of the weight packs on your hips so they fit comfortably and don't restrict your range of motion.
  • You can select the amount of training weight to suit your fitness level and training goals. The Adult size belt includes 8lbs training weight (4lbs in each weight pack) and the Youth size belt includes 4lbs training weight (2lbs in each weight pack).
  • The belt goes on easily and comes off in seconds so players can wear the belt without disrupting practice.
Safety

Works with your pants to minimize the risk of injury:

  • If you fall or collide with another player the belt is designed to transfer the force of the impact onto the protective padding in your pants to minimize the risk of injury.
  • By positioning the training weight around your center of gravity, and not on your back or extremities, the belt helps you avoid unnecessary strain on your lower back and core muscles.
  • While the belt has been designed to protect players in case of a fall or collision it is not recommended to use the belt during drills which focus substantially on body contact. Proper sizing is required to achieve proper protection. Injury may result if the belt is not used as directed. Consult your User Guide for complete instructions on wearing the belt.
