BRONKO BELT RTI

The Bronko Belt RTI is used by a growing number of pro, junior and youth players across North America to help develop functional strength, speed and endurance that directly impacts on-ice performance.

  • Balanced weight distribution around center of gravity
  • Hands free to shoot, pass and stickhandle
  • Easy to use, adjustable weight and full range of motion
  • Works with your pants to minimize the risk of injury
  • High quality materials and sturdy construction for durability
