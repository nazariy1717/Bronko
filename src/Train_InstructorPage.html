DION DEL MONTE

President and Founder, Bronko Hockey Inc.

Dion Del Monte is a professional skating and skills instructor with exceptional skating and puck skills, strong communication capabilities and a strict attention to detail. He's one of the head instructors at Bronko Hockey and has instructed at camps and clinics in Canada, USA, Germany and Latvia.

Dion played NCAA Division 1 hockey at Dartmouth College (Hanover, NH) and graduated in 1995 with a degree in economics. BIOGRAPHY

Dion played NCAA Division 1 hockey at Dartmouth College (Hanover, NH) and graduated in 1995 with a degree in economics. In his final year he was named a co-captain, led the team in scoring, was selected team MVP and was named to the First Team All-Ivy League. He finished his college hockey career listed among the top 20 All-Time Scorers in Dartmouth history.

His career as a professional player began the following year and spanned 13 seasons and over 650 games. He played primarily in Germany (8 seasons, 1 championship) with stops in Italy, England and the US before retiring in 2008.

After his retirement, Dion spent two years researching, developing and patenting a new training tool to help players develop better on-ice power and speed. Bronko Hockey was founded and the Bronko Belt was introduced to players at Bronko Hockey camps and clinics. Word spread as players, coaches and parents learned about the Belt and the benefits of training with it and today, at last count, the Belt is used by players in 9 of 10 provinces and 23 states.

Dion is currently working with several coaches and teams to develop and test a proprietary skating and skills development program that teams and associations will be able to use in conjunction with the Bronko Belt to expedite their players' skating and skills development.

Dion resides with his wife and son in Mississauga.

Playing career highlights
  • Ontario Midget AAA Champion with Toronto Red Wings: Represented Ontario at National Midget AAA Championships.
  • 1 year with Bramalea Blues of Metro Junior A League: League championship finalist, Team Rookie of the Year.
  • 4 years at Dartmouth College, NCAA Div 1, ECAC, Ivy League. Senior year: Co-captain, Team leading scorer, selected Team MVP, First Team All-Ivy League. Finished top 20 All-time scorers at Dartmouth College.
  • 13 years professional hockey in Germany, Italy, England, USA: Over 650 Games, won 1 Championship, one time Finalist.
