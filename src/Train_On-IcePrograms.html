On-Ice Programs

We have a range of training options to help you reach your goals. Contact Us today for pricing and availability.

Team Training
  • Buy a training package of 10 sessions and have a professional skating and skills coach work with your team on your practice ice throughout the season.
  • Develop a custom training plan with us and watch your players work through a challenging progression of exercises designed to teach the specific skills and tactics your team needs.
  • Learn new drill progressions you can use to supplement your normal training so players continue to develop speed, consistency and confidence in skill execution.
  • 2017/18 Pricing: $2000/10x 1hr Sessions (plus HST)
Small group & private training
  • Work with a professional skating and skills coach in a small group or private setting and get the specific information you need to accelerate skill acquistion and improve performance.
  • Individual attention and personalized feedback allows players to make corrections on the spot, learn proper movement patterns more quickly and perform valuable reps using proper technique.
  • Our experienced instructors put the skills being taught into context so players develop an understanding of how to use skills they are learning in game situations to produce maximum results.
  • As players learn to perform skills with speed and consistency their confidence grows and the effectiveness multiplies. Players develop high end skills that transfers into increased on-ice production.
Upcoming camps

We offer a variety of popular youth camps throughout the year for players looking to learn new skills and tactics, develop their decision-making and improve their conditioning.

The cost, age range and emphasis of training will vary from camp to camp but we strive for a maximum ratio of 5 players per instructor on ice.

Most camps are comprised of a skill development component followed by situational game play component so players work to master a skill in isolation and then learn to use the skill in game play where decision making is required.

View Upcoming Camps for details on individual camps.
